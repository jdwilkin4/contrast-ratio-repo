H2 Notes or "How-To-Use" section

Custom color variables were added under the root element. I added section tags for the primary and secondary sections and moved the container div tag inside of each. I added a slight background color to the primary section. I also reduced the border of the pass/fail circles from 4px to 3px. And I gave the footer a slightly lighter color. Finally, I added styling for the input buttons and sized the swatch to match the input height.
